Load attributes via SurfConext

You can now load basic attributes from your home institution (university, HBO, hospital, etc.) onto your IRMA app via SurfConext!

To retrieve your attributes, you first have to login at your home institution via SurfContext (you use your usual login). This allows us to retrieve your attributes. We will then show them to you and give you the option to load them into your IRMA app.
